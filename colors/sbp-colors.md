### Signal colors

These tertiary colors should not be used for general design purposes. They are only intended to serve as signal colors, for example, to indicate positive/negative or true/false in infographics or for other digital communication purposes.

| Color              | Hex       |
|--------------------|-----------|
| True Green         | `#0DEBA5` |
| False Red          | `#FF6D8C` |
